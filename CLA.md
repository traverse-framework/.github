## Why This Exists

Projects under `traverse-framework` are open source, but the project owner wants to preserve the ability to relicense or commercially license the combined codebase in the future without having to track down and get consent from every individual contributor after the fact. This agreement grants that right up front, in exchange for your contribution being included in an open source project under a permissive license (Apache-2.0, unless a given repo states otherwise).

4. **No warranty.** Your contribution is provided "as is," without warranty of any kind, to the extent permitted by applicable law.

## How Acceptance Works

Acceptance is recorded per-contributor, per-organization (not per-repository), typically via an automated CLA-assistant check on your first pull request to any repo in this org. You only need to accept once; it covers future contributions to any `traverse-framework` repository.
